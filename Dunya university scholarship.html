About Dunya University

Dunya University of Afghanistan, founded in 2009, is a prominent private institution located in Kabul. It was established by General Ahmad Ali Sangdel and his sons in partnership with the Swiss UMEF University. The university is officially recognized by Afghanistan's Ministry of Higher Education and is accredited by the Accreditation Council for Business Schools and Programs (ACBSP). Dunya University offers a range of bachelor's and master's degree programs, including Economics, Law and Political Science, and Computer Science. It is renowned for its international faculty, primarily from Europe and North America, who contribute to Afghanistan's academic and professional sectors. The institution also emphasizes practical learning through high-level conferences and seminars. Furthermore, Dunya University played a pioneering role in introducing accreditation standards to higher education in Afghanistan.
